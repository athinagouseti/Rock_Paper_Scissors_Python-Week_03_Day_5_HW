How to Play

  • Two players.
  • Players say "Rock, Paper, Scissors" together with their hands in a fist.
  • On "Scissors", players pick one of three moves to show with their hand: Rock (demonstarted by a fist), Paper (demonstrated by holding out a flat hand), Scissors (demonstrated with two fingers spread similar to a sideways peace sign)
  • if players use the same move, it's a draw.
  • If one player chooses rock and the other scissors, winner is rock (rock crushes scissors).
  • If one player chooses paper and the other rock, winner is paper (paper covers rock).
  • If one player chooses scissors and the other paper, winner is scissors (scissors cut paper)
